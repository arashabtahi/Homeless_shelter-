Diversified Perfection

Our mission statement- Our organization strives to understand the need of underserved populations including homelessness, low income families and children with special needs in an effort to equip them with necessary items for survival and maintaining. The items are included, but not limited to, basic necessities, hygiene products, esteem building, resources and referrals to help better their current situations. We are compassionately dedicated to improving the lives of those in need. Diversified Perfection means we are all diverse and fearfully and wonderfully made in God's image so that has to be perfection! Understanding diversity means we have to understand that we as a people are different due to our cultural background, race, religion and ethnicity but we all live in the same world and we all can learn from eachother.

Blessings, Tashara Callaway
